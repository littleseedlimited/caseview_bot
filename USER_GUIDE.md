## 3. Starting a New Case üìÅ
The core of CaseView is the **Case Folder**.

1. Use the `/newbrief` command.
2. Provide a short **Case Reference** (e.g., *Smith vs. Jones 2024*).
3. **Add Documents**: You can upload PDFs, Word files, or even clear photos of documents.
4. **Media Capture**: Use `/capture` to quickly take a photo or record a voice note for the case if you are on the go.
5. **OCR Text Extraction**: Simply send any image and the bot will automatically extract text for you to review and edit.

## 3.1 OCR Text Extraction üì∏
Text extraction from images is **automatic**. No command needed!

1. Send any photo of a document (scanned page, screenshot, etc.).
2. The bot will automatically extract text and show you a preview.
3. Choose what to do:
   - **Use for New Case** - Create a case from the text.
   - **Copy Text** - Get the full text to copy elsewhere.
   - **Edit Text** - Fix any OCR mistakes before using.
   - **Discard** - Cancel.

## 7. Subscription Plans & Payments üíé
